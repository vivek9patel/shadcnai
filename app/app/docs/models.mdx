## Model Selection Guide

### For Beginners

Start with the default `gemini-1.5-flash` - it's fast, reliable, and produces great results for most use cases.

### For Professionals

Use `claude-3-5-sonnet-20241022` for sophisticated applications requiring deep understanding of design principles.

### For Creatives

Try `gpt-4.1` for innovative, artistic themes that stand out from conventional designs.

### For Rapid Prototyping

Use `llama3.1-70b` or `deepseek-chat` for quick iterations and experimentation.

### For Unique Designs

Experiment with `grok-beta` or `llama-4-scout-17b-16e-instruct` for unconventional approaches.
